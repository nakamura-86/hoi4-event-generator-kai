About HoI4 World News Generator

This is a free online tool designed for the Hearts of Iron IV (HoI4) community. Whether you are a modder creating a new total conversion mod or a player looking to create funny HoI4 memes, our generator allows you to create authentic-looking "World News" events in seconds.

Key Features:

  • Drag & Drop: Easily position your uploaded photos with a mouse or touch.
  • Zoom Control: Scale your images to fit perfectly in the news frame.
  • Black & White Filter: Convert any modern photo into a period-accurate 1930s/40s style image with one click.
  • High-Quality Export: Save your custom event as a high-resolution PNG file ready for use in-game or on social media.

How to use:

  1. Upload Photo: Choose your desired image from your device.
  2. Adjust Image: Use the "Zoom" slider and drag the image directly on the newspaper to position it.
  3. Style: Toggle the "Black & White" filter if you want a classic historical look.
  4. Edit Text: Enter your custom Event Title, News Content, and the Option Button label.
  5. Download: Click "Export as PNG" to save the final image to your computer.
